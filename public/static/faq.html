How to search

Use show 100 to get more results (replace 100 with any number)

Need a specific country? Simply input either its name or country code. You can also search for multiple at once.

Hungry for some fast nodes? Keyword fast adds +100Mbps to the speed requirement, and gigabit +1000Mbps.

Try residential and hosting as well, you already know what they do.

You can also find specific ASNs, for example AS60068.
